Hick's Law

Taylor-Guitars-Website

Hick's Law is nicely shown in this Taylor guitar web page. With the simplicity of the design, and not having to many things to ckick on. On this page, the user is simply trying to decide which guitar series they want to view, and the site makes it nice and simple to find the one you want.

Safe-Austin-Website

This website does a great job using the rule of thirds. For example, if you dray to equally speced lines cuting the photo in thirds, the female is nicely placed on the line for the third. White Space And Design

LG (Life's Good)
LG-Life's-Good-Website

The LG website Exemplifies how white space can help make the site seem more clean and rounded. It shows good design thought with the photo of the TV using the rule of thirds, and then having the text in the other third of the page, nicely centered in that space.
