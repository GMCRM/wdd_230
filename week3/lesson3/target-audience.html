Target audiance description:

This website will be targeting people of all ages in the city of Cameron. It will connect business owners and locals.

Two personas: Jacob is a young Adult who travels to Cameron daily after work to see his girlfriend. Amelia is Jacob's girlfriend. She works at the local diner as a waitress and knows most people in the community from her job.

Five listed scenarios:

1. Amelia checks the calendar to find out what local event just happened that is bringing more people than usual to the diner. Turns out there was an event down the street hosted by a local business, and many people decided that the diner was the best place to eat.
2. Jacob checks the website to find out that a local business is hosting a community dance. He invites Amelia. 3.Amelia wants to connect with local businesses who are holding special events. She goes on the website and learns more.
4. Jacob's mom tells him that she was on the commerce website for camerson and saw that there were some great deals going on for valentines day at the local flower shop, and said he should get Amelia some flowers from there.
5. Amelia goes onto the website to find the best local parks that have a drinking fountain and a bathroom for a picnic she and Jacob are about to go on.
